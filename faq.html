FAQ - Join, ICT, Etc.

  • What is the ICT Club of Kalika?

    The ICT Club of Kalika is a student organization dedicated to promoting and enhancing knowledge and skills in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) among Kalika School students.

  • How do I join the ICT Club?

    To join the ICT Club, you can attend our club meetings, which are usually held on the hall of KMGSS Sector-2. You can also contact our club president or advisor for more information.

  • Is there a membership fee to join the ICT Club?

    The ICT Club typically does not charge a membership fee. However, there may be nominal fees for specific events or workshops.

  • Who is the talented developer behind the creation of the ICT Club Kalika website?

    The ICT Club Kalika website was expertly crafted by Dev.Sujal Gautam, whose exceptional skills and dedication have brought our online platform to life. Their remarkable work has greatly contributed to our digital presence.

  • How many programmers does it take to change a light bulb?

    None. It's a hardware problem.

  • Can I showcase my ICT-related projects on the website?

    Absolutely! We encourage members to share their ICT-related projects and achievements on the website. It's a great way to inspire others and showcase your skills.
